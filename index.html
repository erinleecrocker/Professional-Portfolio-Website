About Me

Erin Crocker is a graphic designer working on acquiring her certification in web development through Georgia Tech's Web Development Bootcamp. With recent changes in the world and economy she is hoping to use her newfound skill sets to become a proficient developer and designer. She has experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


Erin enjoys drawing and painting in her free time, as well as outdoor activities such as; kayaking, biking, and hiking with her son. With her boundless creativity and love of learning every challenge becomes a chance to grow and become more versatile.
